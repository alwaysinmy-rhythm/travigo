Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and support options. If you can't find what you're looking for, please visit our Contact page.

How do I book a trip on Travigo?

Booking a trip on Travigo is easy and can be done in a few simple steps:

  • Enter your destination and travel dates in our search bar
  • Browse through available options for flights, hotels, or complete packages
  • Select the options that best suit your needs and budget
  • Fill in traveler information and any special requirements
  • Review your booking details before proceeding to payment

Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive a confirmation email with all your booking details.

Can I book flights and hotels separately?

Yes, Travigo offers flexible booking options. You can book flights, hotels, car rentals, and activities separately or as a complete package. Our search function allows you to filter specifically for the services you need. However, booking a complete package often comes with special discounts compared to booking services individually.

What information do I need to make a booking?

To complete a booking on Travigo, you'll need:

  • Full names of all travelers (exactly as they appear on ID/passport)
  • Birth dates for all travelers
  • Contact information (email and phone number)
  • Payment details
  • For international travel: passport information (number, expiration date, issuing country)

Additional information may be required depending on your destination and the services you're booking.

What types of travel services does Travigo offer?

Travigo offers a comprehensive range of travel services including:

  • Flight bookings (domestic and international)
  • Hotel accommodations (from budget to luxury)
  • Vacation packages and all-inclusive resorts
  • Car rentals worldwide
  • Cruises and cruise packages
  • Tours and local experiences
  • Travel insurance
  • Airport transfers

We partner with thousands of providers worldwide to offer you the best selection and prices.

How can I find the best deals on Travigo?

To find the best deals on Travigo:

  • Use our "Flexible Dates" search option to see prices across different days
  • Sign up for our newsletter to receive exclusive promotions and flash deals
  • Check our "Deals" section regularly for limited-time offers
  • Book vacation packages rather than individual services for bundle discounts
  • Consider traveling during shoulder seasons (just before or after peak season)
  • Create a Travigo account to access member-only prices and earn travel rewards

We also offer a price match guarantee if you find a lower price for the same booking elsewhere.

What payment methods do you accept?

Travigo accepts multiple payment methods including:

  • All major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Bank transfers (for select bookings)
  • Travigo gift cards and travel credits

Payment options may vary slightly depending on your country and the specific booking.

Does Travigo offer payment plans or book now, pay later options?

Yes, Travigo offers flexible payment options for eligible bookings:

  • Deposit payments: Pay a portion upfront and the remainder closer to your travel date
  • Monthly installments: Split your payment into equal monthly payments (available for bookings over $300)
  • Book now, pay later: Reserve your trip now and pay in full 30 days before departure

Eligibility for these options depends on the type of booking, total amount, and how far in advance you're booking. Available payment plans will be shown during checkout.

Are there any hidden fees when booking with Travigo?

At Travigo, we believe in transparent pricing. The prices you see include all mandatory fees and taxes. However, some additional charges may apply:

  • Resort or city fees (charged directly by the accommodation)
  • Optional travel insurance
  • Baggage fees for flights (varies by airline)
  • Seat selection fees (for some airlines)
  • Currency conversion fees (for international payments)

All applicable fees will be clearly displayed before you complete your booking so there are no surprises.

How do I contact Travigo customer support?

Travigo customer support can be reached through multiple channels:

  • Phone: +1 (800) 123-4567 (available 24/7)
  • Email: support@travigo.com
  • Live Chat: Available on our website and mobile app
  • Help Center: Browse our extensive knowledge base
  • Social Media: Message us on Facebook or Twitter

For urgent matters related to a current trip, we recommend calling our 24/7 support line for immediate assistance.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

Our cancellation and refund policies vary depending on the service booked and the provider's terms:

  • Flights: Most airlines offer limited refunds. Many tickets allow changes for a fee.
  • Hotels: Many offer free cancellation up to 24-48 hours before check-in.
  • Vacation packages: Usually refundable 30+ days before departure with a cancellation fee.
  • Activities: Often refundable up to 24 hours before the scheduled time.

The specific cancellation policy for your booking will be clearly displayed before purchase and in your confirmation email. For maximum flexibility, consider booking options with free cancellation or adding travel insurance to your booking.

Does Travigo offer travel insurance?

Yes, Travigo offers comprehensive travel insurance options through our trusted partners. Our travel insurance plans typically include coverage for:

  • Trip cancellation and interruption
  • Emergency medical expenses and evacuation
  • Baggage loss or delay
  • Travel delays and missed connections
  • 24/7 emergency assistance

Insurance options are presented during the booking process with different levels of coverage to suit your needs. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance, especially for international trips or significant bookings.
